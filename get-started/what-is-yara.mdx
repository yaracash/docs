Yara is stablecoin infrastructure. It lets businesses integrate stablecoin rails — wallets, ramps, USD accounts, and liquidity — directly into their own products and workflows.

If you're building financial products for Africa, Yara gives you reliable access to the building blocks without building the underlying infrastructure yourself.

## The problem we solve

Working with stablecoins usually means stitching together wallet infrastructure, blockchain rails, payment processors, bank connections, and liquidity providers. Each one is its own integration, its own risk, and its own maintenance burden.

Yara abstracts all of that behind one API. You get simple endpoints; we handle the complexity underneath.

## What you can do

- **Create and manage wallets** for users, businesses, or internal treasury.
- **Move money between fiat and stablecoins** in supported African markets.
- **Receive funds** through USD virtual accounts.
- **Access liquidity** for stablecoin operations.
- **Power** treasury, settlement, payouts, and embedded finance use cases.

## Who it's for

Yara is infrastructure for businesses, fintechs, platforms, and financial operators that want to embed stablecoin capabilities — without managing wallets, ramps, bank rails, liquidity providers, or blockchain complexity themselves.
