Yara gives businesses building in Africa direct access to stablecoin rails — wallets, fiat ramps, USD virtual accounts, and liquidity — through one simple, developer-friendly API.

We handle the wallets, blockchain rails, payment flows, and liquidity sourcing. You focus on your product.
